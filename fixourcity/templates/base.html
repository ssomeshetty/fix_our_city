Our Platform Features

Issue Reporting

Users can report issues directly through the platform, providing clear descriptions and location markers.

Role-based Access

The platform offers role-specific dashboards for Public, Authority, Contractor, and Admin users with tailored functionalities.

Upvoting System

Users can upvote issues to increase visibility. Issues with 50+ upvotes are automatically escalated to Authorities.

Issue Resolution Tracking

Track the status of reported issues as they move from "New" to "Pending" and finally to "Completed" once resolved by contractors.

Contractor Assignment

Authorities can assign contractors to issues, and contractors can update the issue status to "Completed" once resolved.

Admin Management

Admins have complete control over the platform, including user management, issue monitoring, and overall platform maintenance.

How It Works

Public Users

Report issues, upvote existing problems, and track progress on community improvements in your area.

  • Submit detailed reports
  • Add photos and location data
  • Track issue status updates

Authorities

Review high-priority issues and assign contractors for swift resolution based on urgency and impact.

  • Dashboard of all reported issues
  • Prioritize by urgency and votes
  • Assign to specialized contractors

Contractors

Access assigned tasks and update progress as repairs are completed with detailed documentation.

  • Manage work orders efficiently
  • Update work status in real-time
  • Track performance metrics

Admins

Manage platform operations and ensure smooth coordination between all stakeholders.

  • System-wide oversight
  • User account management
  • Optimize resolution workflow
